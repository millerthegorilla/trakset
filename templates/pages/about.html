How to use the {{ APP_USER }} assets app...



Open the camera on your phone, or your QR code scanner app, and scan the QR Code on the item you wish to transfer to your name.


The app or the camera will present you with a web address, that you can visit. Click the web address to open it, and sign in to the assets app if you are not already signed in. When you sign in, click the checkbox that is labelled 'Remember me', so that you won't have to sign in again.

As soon as you are signed in, the asset will be transferred to you. If you want to cancel the transfer, click the 'cancel' button, and confirm, and the transfer will be cancelled. If you have left the page and you want to cancel the transfer, just scan the QR code again within {{ TRANSFER_TIMEOUT }} hours of making the transfer, and you will be able to cancel the transfer from the page you visit.

You can also add notes when you are making a transfer, or within {{ TRANSFER_TIMEOUT }} hours, return to the transfer and update the notes attached to the transfer.



What should I do if I don't have a phone?



If you don't have a phone, or your phone doesn't scan QR codes, then ask the person that you are transferring from to scan the QR code, and add a note to the transfer naming you as the current holder e.g.

'Bob has received this key, but doesn't have a phone'
